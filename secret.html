The 20-Day Secret

Here's exactly how to make your avocados last 4x longer

1

Buy at Different Ripeness Levels

Get 2-3 rock-hard avocados and 1-2 that are almost ripe. This staggers your supply so you always have one ready.

2

Ripen at Room Temperature

Keep unripe avocados on the counter. Want to speed it up? Put them in a paper bag with a banana ‚Äî the ethylene gas accelerates ripening.

3

Refrigerate When "Just Right"

The moment an avocado gives slightly to gentle pressure, move it to the fridge. This is the key step most people miss! Cold storage pauses ripening almost completely.

4

Store Properly in the Fridge

Place whole avocados in the crisper drawer. They'll stay perfect for up to 2 weeks at this stage. No bags needed for whole avocados.

5

For Cut Avocados: The Pit Trick

Keep the pit in the unused half, squeeze lemon/lime juice on the flesh, press plastic wrap directly onto the surface (no air gaps!), then refrigerate. Lasts 2-3 more days.

For cut avocado halves, submerge them face-down in cold water in an airtight container. The water blocks oxygen completely. Some people report 4-5 days of freshness this way!
